Demo 1

In this demo, I create a group of balls with the material of type Phong. It is similar to the example we did in class, however, I decide to add interactive effect to it. As you clicked on the canvas, the balls would receive an arbitrary force and getting `pushed` somewhere else.

Demo 2

In this demo, I explore more about fundamental three.js . I experiment with other geometry type such as plane and box. I also make use of the Axes Helper, which helps me determine the 3D position . To interact with the visuals, click on the canvas to give those cubes an arbitrary forces and make them accelerate.

I also utilize the `fog` effect. Use your mouse's scroll wheel to zoom in. The objects that appear further in the scene would have a mist above. I find it very interesting.
